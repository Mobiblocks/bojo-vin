Velicinsky Gold (Sweet)

  • Description

    Alcohol: min 10,0 %, sugar 50 mg/dm3,made from Vitis Vinifera L white grapes species, that were subjected to a short partial maceration, liqueur d’expedition, impregnated with carbon dioxide.

    Sparkling wine “Velicinsky” Gold is the perfect combination of an elegant look and sensual aroma. It is a fine bouquet of the most famous European grape varieties- Aligote, Chardonnay, Sauvignon.

    Frothy bublees, a slight hint of sweet peper, quince and blackurrant notes on the background and a wild floral nuance can describe its fascinating taste.
