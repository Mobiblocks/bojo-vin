About Company

BOJO-VIN” is a manufacturer of vodka, soft alcoholic drinks and sparkling wines. We are located in the Republic of Moldova, Vatra village. At the factory we have implemented quality management systems as SR EN ISO 9001: 2015, and SR ISO 22000, which guarantee the high quality of the products.

"This drink has a magical power. It strengthens the weak, and revives those who have fainted. Those tired after work and physical activity can return their life forces by this drink much sooner than by nourishment. … It works as a diuretic, an appetizer, an antitoxin."

- Carolus Linnaeus (18th-century physician documenting the effects of the drink) -

There are four production lines installed , with a capacity of more than 7 million bottles per month. Currently we have a 30% share of the Moldovan market in spirit drinks, we have over 100 highly skilled employees and an experience of over 14 years in the market.

At present the company has more than 20 trademarks holding more than 80 original vodka recipes, low alcohol drinks, special vodkas, liquors and sparkling wines.
