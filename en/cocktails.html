Frizzante Strawberry
+ Ingredients
  • 3/4 cup Russian Admiral Vodka
  • 1 btl Valahia Muscat sparkling wine
  • 4 cups fresh sliced strawberries
  • 3 tbsps sugar
  • 5 sprigs fresh basil (garnish)
Preparation

1Place the fruit, sugar, and basil in a large pitcher. Using a wooden spoon, crush the mixture until it forms a rough mash.


2Stir in the vodka Russian Admiral. Just before serving, pour the Valahia Muscat Sparkling Wine over the mixture and serve immediately over ice.

Cactus Margarita
+ Ingredients
  • One 11,15 oz bottle Party Juice Cactus
  • 1 lime wedge
  • kosher salt
  • ice
  • 1 ounce tequila (silver)
  • 1 ounce fresh lime juice
Preparation

1Moisten half of the outer rim of a glass with the lime wedge and twist the edge into a saucer full of kosher salt.


2Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add the tequila, lime juice. Shake well and strain into the glass. Top with Party Juice Cactus.

PJ Mojito
+ Ingredients
  • One 11,15 oz bottle Party Juice Mojito
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 6-8 mint leaves
  • 1 lime, halved
  • 1 ounce light rum
  • Mint sprig for garnish
Preparation

1Place the sugar, the mint leaves, and Party Juice Mojito into a highball glass. Muddle well to dissolve the sugar and to release the mint flavor.


2Squeeze the juice from both halves of the lime into the glass. Drop one half of the lime into the glass.


3Add the rum and stir well. Fill the glass with ice cubes.

PJ Pink Paloma
+ Ingredients
  • One 11,15 oz bottle Party Juice Grapefruit
  • Wedges of grapefruit
  • 1/4 cup pink grapefruit juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1 ounce silver tequila
Preparation

1In a cocktail shaker filled with ice add the tequila and the pink grapefruit juice. Shake until chilled.


2Squeeze the grapefruit from a wedge into the drink and stir. Top with Party Juice Grapefruit, and serve with an additional grapefruit wedge for garnish.
